Who We Are

We are August and Malachi, two web developers and designers collaborating to bring exceptional website solutions to Indianapolis small businesses. With our combined expertise in design, development, and digital strategy, we help local businesses establish a strong online presence.

Our flexible collaboration approach allows us to adapt to each project's unique needs, ensuring you get the right combination of skills and attention. We work closely with each client to understand their business goals and create websites that truly represent their brand.
