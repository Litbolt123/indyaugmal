Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on the tier you choose and project complexity. Silver Tier projects typically take 1-2 weeks, Gold Tier takes 3-4 weeks, and Platinum Tier takes 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your specific needs.

What's included in each pricing tier?

Each tier includes different features and pages. Silver Tier is a single-page website perfect for getting started. Gold Tier includes up to 5 pages with more features. Platinum Tier is unlimited pages with advanced custom features. See our Services page for full details.

Do I need to provide content and images?

We can work with content and images you provide, or we can help source professional stock images and write content for you. For Platinum Tier projects, we provide professional photography guidance. We'll discuss your preferences during the initial consultation.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All our websites are built mobile-first and are fully responsive. They'll look great and function perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile optimization is included in every tier.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes! Gold and Platinum tiers include a content management system (CMS) that allows you to easily update content, add blog posts, and manage your site without technical knowledge. We'll provide training on how to use it.

What about SEO and search engine optimization?

Basic SEO is included in all tiers. This includes proper page structure, meta tags, and mobile optimization. Gold and Platinum tiers include more advanced SEO features like Google Analytics setup, Search Console integration, and content optimization.

What happens after my website launches?

We provide post-launch support! Silver Tier includes launch support. Gold Tier includes 1 month of post-launch support. Platinum Tier includes 3 months of support. This covers bug fixes, minor updates, and answering questions about your site.

Do you provide hosting?

We can help you set up hosting or recommend reliable hosting providers. Many clients choose to host on platforms like GitHub Pages (free), Netlify, or traditional hosting. We'll guide you through the process.

What's your revision policy?

Silver Tier includes 1 round of revisions. Gold Tier includes 2 rounds. Platinum Tier includes unlimited revisions during the development phase. We want to make sure you're completely happy with your website before launch!

Can you integrate with my existing tools?

Yes! We can integrate with various third-party tools like booking systems, payment processors, email marketing platforms, and more. Let us know what you need during the consultation, and we'll discuss integration options.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

We offer ongoing maintenance and update services. After your included support period ends, we can provide hourly rates for updates, or we can discuss a monthly maintenance plan. We're here to help your website grow with your business.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our contact form or reach out via email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs, answer any questions, and provide a detailed proposal. No obligation!

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! Get in touch and we'll answer any questions about your project.
